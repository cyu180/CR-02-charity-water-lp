Our Goals

At Charity Water, our mission is simple — to bring clean, safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Millions of families spend hours each day walking to collect dirty water. We’re changing that by building sustainable water wells and systems.

With your support, we aim to provide clean water to 100 million people by 2030. Together, we can make a lasting impact on health, education, and economic development in communities around the world.

Overview

Every drop creates a measurable change. Charity water is here to help the people in need to get clean water. With your help, we have funded over 60,000 water projects in 29 countries, providing clean water to over 12 million people.
